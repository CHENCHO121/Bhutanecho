Literature & Arts Trust Fund The Literature and Arts Trust Fund of Bhutan is a proactive festival fund established in accordance with the Royal Vision of Her Majesty The Queen Mother Gyalyum Azhi Dorji Wangmo Wangchuck. Her Majesty understood the great importance of Literature for the holistic, sustainable development Gross National Happiness has to offer to her people. To this end, Her Majesty envisioned to create an inclusive platform for literature and arts for the creation of an enlightened society in pursuit of collective progress and happiness.
